How it works

Implementing a white-labelled platform

You pick from a range of validated mental health and wellbeing assessment questionnaires, choose what existing resources and support will be pre-loaded into the platform and we will customise the platform for you.

App and admin analytics access

Students receive access to Syndi’s app, available on both Android and iOS and administrators receive access to an analytics dashboard that provides an overview of assessment responses and resource utilisation.

Assessment and navigation to support

Students are prompted via SMS to complete weekly, bi-weekly or monthly assessments within the app. Students build awareness of their mental wellbeing through their own secure dashboard. Personalised signposting to support resources are suggested based on each student’s needs and engagement preferences.
